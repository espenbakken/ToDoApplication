This is a Project Management tool to assist you or your study group to organize work and manage projects and tasks.

In this tool you can:

  • Set a deadline for your project
  • Edit and add tasks
  • Add new member and show their role
  • Define your own status categories
  • With drag&drop you can show current status of your task
  • You can assign member a task with drag&drop
  • Follow your progress in diagram
  • Reset and start a new project

Enjoy and good luck with your project!
